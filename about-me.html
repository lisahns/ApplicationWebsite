Hello! I'm Lisa Hanes (she/her) and I am hoping to pursue a career in tech. I grew up in Germany and have been living in London for nearly four years and consider it my new home. Before coming to London, I went backpacking in the USA and Mexico, where I volunteered for all sorts of different companies and charities. Soon I will be graduating from the University of Westminster with a BA (Hons) in Photography.

I had my first coding experience last year, where I attended an 8-week introduction course hosted by Code First Girls. I really enjoyed learning about programming and being able to solve problems and build projects. This made me commit to becoming a web developer. Over the past few months, I have learned a lot about HTML, CSS and JavaScript with the help of friends, the weekly meetups organised by FaC and the discord channel.

I really enjoy the peer-led learning approach that Founders and Coders practices. Not only is it a great pedagogical method which makes learning so much more enjoyable and fun, but it is also fantastic opportunity to develop a set of other skills, such as working in a team or communication skills, which will be relevant in my future career. I am confident that my skills and enthusiasm will allow me to make the most of my time with FaC. I am looking forward to the opportunity to be a part of the FaC community and work alongside other talented people.
